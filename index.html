A little something about me, eh?
My name is Reed. I was born in Vancouver, WA but didn't stay there long. I have moved around my entire life to the point where the entire Northwest feels like home. During my time at college I acquired an Associates Degree in Applied Science and Arts. Immediately after, I started my professional life out as a young Firefighter EMT working with one of the busiest fire departments in the state of WA. After four years of hard work, and a disturbing amount of training, I received several musculoskeletal injuries in a freak car accident(pretty lame, hu!).
This is where coding comes in.

Back in July 2015 I wrote my first line of code in Seattle. While recovering from my injuries in early fall, I found solace in continuing to learn how to code and never stopped.
I am hoping that my career as a developer gives me the resources as well as the freedom to continue to reach my goals and travel the world.
