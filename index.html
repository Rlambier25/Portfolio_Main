A little something about me, eh?
My name is Reed. I was born in Vancouver, WA but didn't stay there long. I have moved around my entire life all over to the point where the entire Northwest feels like home. I have an Associates Degree in Applied Science and Arts. I LOVE to travel and explore the world. I started my professsional life out as a young Firefighter EMT working with one of the busiest fire departments in the state of WA. After four years of hard work, and a disturbing amount of training, I recieved several musculoskeletal injuries in a freak car accident. This is where coding comes in. Back in July 2015 I wrote my first line of code in Seattle. While recovering from my injuries in early fall, I found solice in continuing to learn how to code.
I am hoping that my career as a software engineer gives me the resources as well as the freedom to live my life the way I have always wanted.
